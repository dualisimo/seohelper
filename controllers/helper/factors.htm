Search engine optimization — SEO — seems like alchemy to the uninitiated. But there's a science to it. Below are some important "ranking factors" and best practices that can lead to success with both search engines and searchers.

All factors on the table are important, but those marked 3 carry more weight than 1 or 2. No single factor guarantees top rankings or success, but having several favorable ones increases the odds. Negative “violation” factors shown in red harm your chances.

ON-THE-PAGE SEO

Content

  • QUALITY+3 Are pages well written & have substantial quality content?
  • RESEARCH+3 Have you researched the keywords people may use to find your content?
  • WORDS+2 Do pages use words & phrases you hope they'll be found for?
  • FRESH+2 Are pages fresh & about "hot" topics?
  • VERTICAL+2 Do you have image, local, news, video or other vertical content?
  • ANSWERS+1 Is your content turned into direct answers within search results?
  • THIN-2 Is content "thin" or "shallow" & lacking substance?

Architecture

  • CRAWL+3 Can search engines easily "crawl" pages on site?
  • DUPLICATE+2 Does site manage duplicate content issues well?
  • MOBILE+2 Does your site work well for mobile devices & make use of app indexing?
  • SPEED+1 Does site load quickly?
  • URLS+1 Do URLs contain meaningful keywords to page topics?
  • HTTPS+1 Does site use HTTPS to provide secure connection for visitors?
  • CLOAKING-3 Do you show search engines different pages than humans?

HTML

  • TITLES+3 Do HTML title tags contain keywords relevant to page topics?
  • DESCRIPTION+2 Do meta description tags describe what pages are about?
  • STRUCTURE+2 Do pages use structured data to enhance listings?
  • HEADERS+1 Do headlines & subheads use header tags with relevant keywords?
  • STUFFING-2 Do you excessively use words you want pages to be found for?
  • HIDDEN-1 Do colors or design "hide" words you want pages to be found for?

OFF-THE-PAGE SEO

TRUST

  • AUTHORITY+3 Do links, shares & other factors make site a trusted authority?
  • ENGAGE+2 Do visitors spend time reading or "bounce" away quickly?
  • HISTORY+2 Has site or its domain been around a long time, operating in same way?
  • IDENTITY+1 Does site use means to verify its identity & that of authors?
  • PIRACY-1 Has site been flagged for hosting pirated content?
  • ADS-1 Is your content ad-heavy, especially “above-the-fold”?

LINKS

  • QUALITY+3 Are links from trusted, quality or respected web sites?
  • TEXT+2 Do links pointing at pages use words you hope they'll be found for?
  • NUMBER+1 Do many links point at your web pages?
  • PAID-3 Have you purchased links in hopes of better rankings?
  • SPAM-3 Have you created links by spamming blogs, forums or other places?

PERSONAL

  • COUNTRY+3 What country is someone located in?
  • LOCALITY+3 What city or local area is someone located in?
  • HISTORY+3 Has someone regularly visited your site or socially favored it?
  • SOCIAL+2 Has someone or their friends socially favored the site?

SOCIAL

  • REPUTATION+2 Do those respected on social networks share your content?
  • SHARES+1 Do many share your content on social networks?
